FM Transmitter BA1404

Dive into the world of RF(radio frequency) with the BA1404 HI-FI Stereo transmitter. You will be able to transmit MP3 music from your iPod, computer, discman, walkman, TV/SAT receiver, and many other audio sources! The goal of this project was to create a professional PCB and use surface mount components to help increase the range and quality of the RF signal.

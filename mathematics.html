Example Problems



1. Single Conversion Factors

1.1) How many grams are in 5,700mg of Ag?

\(\ 5,700mg \times \cfrac{10^{-3}g}{1mg} = 5.7g\)

1.2) How many grams are in 0.345 ounces of NaOH?

\(\ 0.345oz \times \cfrac{1g}{0.03527oz} = 9.78g\)



2. Two or more Conversion Facotrs

2.1) How many mL are in 13.45 qts?

\(\ 13.450qt \times \cfrac{1L}{1.057qt} \times \cfrac{10^3mL}{1L} = 12725mL\)
